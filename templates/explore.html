PROJECT WORKING


Step 1: Sign In

Begin by signing in on the VisionMetrics website. Provide the alloted credentials.


Step 2: Dashboard Access

Upon successful sign-in, you will get an access to VisionMetrics dashboard through your account.


Step 3: Accessing Live Video

Live video feed is used containing analog gauges that you want to monitor using VisionMetrics. It also supports various formats for convenience.


Step 4: Automatic Processing

VisionMetrics will automatically process the live video feed content using advanced computer vision techniques. The system identifies, recognizes, and calibrates diverse gauge types.


Step 5: Real-time Insights

Gain instant access to real-time insights derived from the processed gauge data.


Step 6: Receive Alerts (Optional)

If desired, set up alerts for specific threshold values. VisionMetrics will notify you instantly when gauge readings breach predefined limits, enabling proactive intervention.


Step 7: Log Out

Once you have successfully utilized VisionMetrics, log out of your account for security.
